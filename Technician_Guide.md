# 🚀 Safety Home Services - Technician App Guide

Welcome to the **SHS Technician Tracker**! This app is designed to help you track your weekly performance, see your bonuses in real-time, and compete on the leaderboard. 

Here is your step-by-step guide on how to get started and get the most out of the app.

---

## 1. How to Log In
1. Open the app link on your phone's web browser: **[Insert Your App Link Here, e.g., shs-technician-app.vercel.app]**
2. You will see the **Technician Login** screen.
3. Enter your unique **Employee ID** (for example: `MS-001`). 
    * *Note: If you don't know your ID, ask your supervisor!*
4. Tap **Access Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Understanding Your Dashboard
As soon as you log in, you'll see your main dashboard. This updates instantly whenever the admin team enters your weekly numbers.

* **🔥 The Streak:** Next to your initials at the top right, you'll see a fire icon and a number. This represents how many consecutive weeks you've hit your revenue goals!
* **💰 The Big Ring (Current Revenue):** This shows your total revenue for the current week. The ring fills up as you get closer to the **$7,000 bonus threshold**. Once you hit $7,000, it turns green, and your Change Makers Bonus is unlocked!
* **📊 Bonus Calculator:** Right below the ring, you can see exactly how much bonus money you've earned this week (Base Bonus + SPIFs).
* **✅ Key Metrics:** You'll see four quick stats: Jobs Done, your Average Ticket size, 5-Star Reviews collected, and Club Memberships sold.

---

## 3. Weekly Goals & Historical Chart
Scroll down to see your progress over time:
* **Historical Chart:** This bar chart shows your revenue and completed jobs for the last 4 weeks. Use this to see your trends!
* **Weekly Goals:** Here you can see your specific revenue target for the current week and exactly how far away you are from hitting it.

---

## 4. The Leaderboard (Rank View)
Want to see how you stack up against the rest of the team?
1. Look at the **bottom navigation bar** fixed to the bottom of your screen.
2. Tap the **Rank** icon (the ribbon medal).
3. This scrolls you straight to the **Weekly Leaderboard**. 
4. The top 3 performers get special Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals next to their names. See if you can take the #1 spot this week!

---

## 5. Compliance Alerts (Strikes)
We want everyone to get their full bonus! However, if there is a compliance issue (like a dirty van, late paperwork, or a callback), you will see an alert on your dashboard.

* **⚠️ Strike 1:** Shows a yellow warning and deducts $25 from your bonus.
* **🔶 Strike 2:** Shows an orange warning and deducts $75 from your bonus.
* **🚫 Strike 3+ (Disqualified):** Shows a red alert. If you hit 3 strikes in a single week, you forfeit your bonus for that week.
* *How to fix it:* If you see a strike you don't understand, talk to your supervisor immediately to resolve the issue! Strikes reset every Monday morning.

---

## 6. The Trophy Case (Badges)
As you hit major milestones (like selling 100 memberships or getting 50 five-star reviews), you will permanently unlock Badges!
* Scroll down to the **Trophy Case** section to see which badges you've earned.
* Tap on a locked badge (the grayed-out ones) to see exactly what you need to do to unlock it.

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip for iPhone/Android Users
You can save this app directly to your phone's home screen so it acts exactly like a native app!
* **iPhone (Safari):** Tap the "Share" button at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up), scroll down, and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
* **Android (Chrome):** Tap the 3 dots in the top right corner, and tap **"Add to Home screen"**.

---
*If you have any issues logging in or notice any numbers look incorrect, completely close the app and reopen it, or contact your admin team.*
