# SHS Technician Onboarding & App Guide

Welcome to the Safety Home Services family! We believe in rewarding our technicians for outstanding performance. To help you track your progress, bonuses, and goals, we’ve built the **SHS Technician App**.

This document will walk you through exactly how our bonus structure works and how to use the app to maximize your earnings.

---

## Part 1: How You Earn Your Bonus

Your bonus is calculated every week based on three things: **Revenue**, **Extra Incentives (SPIFs)**, and **Compliance**. Every week is a fresh start!

### 1. Base Revenue Bonus
To qualify for the base bonus, you must hit a minimum of **$7,000 in revenue** for the week.
* **$7,000 to $12,999:** You earn **1%** of your total revenue.
* **$13,000+ (Change Makers Elite):** You earn **2%** of your total revenue.

*(Example: If you bring in $8,000 in a week, your base bonus is $80. If you bring in $15,000, your base bonus jumps to $300!)*

### 2. Extra Incentives (SPIFs)
On top of your revenue bonus, you get flat-rate bonuses for specific achievements:
* **$25** for every **5-Star Review** you collect.
* **$25** for every **Club Membership** you sell.

### 3. Compliance (Keeping Your Bonus)
We hold our team to a high standard of professionalism and safety. To keep 100% of your hard-earned bonus, you must pass the weekly 10-point compliance check. The checks include:
* Van Cleanliness
* Paperwork Submitted on Time
* Estimate Follow-ups Completed
* Zero Callbacks
* No Customer Complaints
* Safe Driving (No GPS alerts)
* Passing Drug Screening
* No OSHA Violations
* PACE Training Completed
* Dress Code Followed

**The Strike System:**
If you fail a compliance check, you receive a "Strike" for the week.
* **Strike 1:** $25 deduction from your bonus.
* **Strike 2:** $75 total deduction.
* **Strike 3:** You forfeit your entire bonus for that week.

## Part 2: Using the SHS App

The SHS App is your live scoreboard. It updates automatically when the admin team enters your weekly numbers. 

### How to Log In
1. Open the app link on your phone: **https://safetyhomeservices.app**
2. Enter your **Employee ID**. *(If you don't know your ID, ask your supervisor!)*
3. Tap **Access Dashboard**.

*(Pro-Tip: Tap “Share” in Safari or “Menu” in Chrome to **Add to Home Screen** so it works like a normal app!)*

### Understanding Your Dashboard
* **The Progress Ring:** This big circle tracks your revenue. Watch it fill up as you approach the green $7,000 goal!
* **Bonus Calculator:** Live math showing exactly what you’ll take home this week.
* **Key Metrics:** Quick stats on your Jobs, Average Ticket, Reviews, and Memberships.
* **Compliance Alerts:** If you receive a compliance strike, a warning box will appear here showing exactly what went wrong and how much it cost you. Talk to your supervisor ASAP if you see this!

### Competing on the Leaderboard
Want to see who's bringing in the most revenue this week?
1. Tap the **Rank** button (the small medal icon) on the bottom navigation bar.
2. You’ll see the Top 5 technicians for the week, complete with gold, silver, and bronze medals. 

### Unlocking Badges
Scroll down to your **Trophy Case** to see your lifetime achievements. You earn permanent badges for hitting major milestones (like selling 100 memberships or maintaining a 12-week streak). Tap any locked badge to see what it takes to earn it.
